About My Naval Career Page

Welcome to my Naval Career page! This site is dedicated to showcasing the highlights of my service in the United States Navy. Here, you will find detailed accounts of the ports I've visited, my command history, and significant milestones in my career. Through this page, I hope to share my experiences and provide insight into life in the Navy. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy learning about my journey.
