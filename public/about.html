Welcome to my Navy Page!


This page is dedicated to a lifetime of adventure! It is intended to display items from a 22 year career.


The ultimate goal is to display photos, country information, ship trivia, and more. Keeping my fingers crossed as the scope is quite large.


Eventually, the website could be used not just to hold my memories, but help inform people of why the Navy does what it does and show the importance of various regions of the world and how they affect our lives at home.


At the very least, it will be a repository many memories.
